About Me

Whatever description I write here is bound to be incomplete. We all are evolving, and as we evolve some false self of ours withers away. That is the beauty of this journey. It’s a mystery, a never-ending exploration of joy and peace, of ourselves, getting closer and closer to our source. What we do, the way we express originates from that source. For me, it’s important not to get lost in expression itself, as what lies behind the expression is the essence of art, of all.
